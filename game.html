Crystal Collector

Collect crystals and avoid meteors!

How to Play

Movement

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the game board.

Objective

Collect as many crystals (blue gems) as possible while avoiding meteors!

Crystals

Blue hexagon-shaped crystals spawn randomly on the board. Walk over them to collect them and increase your score.

Meteors

Red squares fall from the sky. You'll get a warning before they land. Avoid being in the impact zone when they hit!

Damage System

If a meteor hits you, you'll be paralyzed and lose points. The damage depends on your current score:

Score Range Paralysis Duration Points Lost
0-10 1 second 1 point
11-20 2 seconds 3 points
21-50 3 seconds 5 points
51+ 5 seconds 7 points

Difficulty Progression

As you collect more crystals, more meteors will start spawning, making the game progressively harder!

Good luck, and have fun!
